OpenCV & WebRTC

Throwback to my work as a student during FSMTU's XXIX Software Fair, where I worked as a Backend Developer for the coding of a Real-Time Video App. Here, I am once again deploying a WebSocket instance as a Signaling Server for the management of RTC Peer Connections. Only one RTC Peer may be working at a time with a timeout of 30 seconds (the connection might take around 30~45 seconds to establish). The idea of the video transmition, is to transform the user's webcam through an OpenCV media transformer.
